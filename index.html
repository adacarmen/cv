About Me

I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Otago. My research sits at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Mixed Reality, and Virtual Tourism.

Before joining Otago, I worked at Huawei as a UX Designer and Product Manager, gaining cross-functional experience across design and product development.

Following that, I contributed to experience design at ZhiYuan robotics company.

If you are interested in my work, feel free to reach out via LinkedIn.
